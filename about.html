--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About I'm a third-year PhD student in the Climate-Ocean-Atmosphere Program at [Scripps Institution of Oceanography][scripps], University of California, San Diego. As an oceanographer, I strive to understand the physical aspects of the ocean and its role in the climate system. I grew up in São Paulo in the southeast of Brazil. I went to school at [University of Sao Paulo][usp] (USP), where I've met awesome friends and I fell in love with applied mathematics, data science, and free software. At USP I also developed a strong sense of fairness and social justice. This may sound cliché but I'm committed to apply my skills to improve the world. In my free time I enjoy listening to music, reading, biking, surfing, and swimming. I'm a big fan of Jorge Ben, jazzy Bossa Nova, piano concerts, Portuguese literature, and The New Yorker.
